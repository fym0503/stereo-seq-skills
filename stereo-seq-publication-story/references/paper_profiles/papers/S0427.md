Figure-By-Figure Caption Digest:
- Figure 1.: The overall framework of spaMMCL. (A) Workflow of spaMMCL. spaMMCL uses multi-modal data as input. The framework comprises two modules: MML and MGL. (B) MML module. The MML module is designed to learn from multi-modal data. It includes three components: (a) modality bias mitigation with feature mask-like strategy, (b) multi-modal features fusion with joint learning, and (c) noise mitigation with graph self-supervised learning. (C) MGL module. The MGL module employs a granularity-guided approach. It includes four components: (a) domain partitioning strategy, (b) fine-grained screening strategy, (c) coarse-grained screening strategy, and (d) granularity-supplemented constraint strategy.
