Both the original and augmented graphs (masked and shuffled graphs) are passed through a shared GNN encoder for representation learning. The latent representation from the masked graph is used for the masked feature reconstruction task, while the representation from the shuffled graph supports the node discrimination task, contributing to model optimization.
