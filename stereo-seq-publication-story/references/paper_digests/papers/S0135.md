Figure-By-Figure Caption Digest:
- Figure 1.: Schematic overview of the STellaris workflow. STellaris is built upon a collection of manually curated ST data, consisting of 101 datasets and 823 sections from humans and mice, which can be accessed and visualized in the dataset browser . The major function is spatial mapping , which takes user-uploaded single-cell omics data and performs a screening analysis using the MIA approach. Then, the scRNA-seq data and the selected ST data are integrated into a shared latent space, where a multivariate RF model is fitted to map cells to spatial locations. Based on the spatial cellular map, spatial proximity between cell types are characterized, followed by the identification of LRIs. Additionally, with the gene search tool, users can search for spatial expression patterns of genes of interest.
