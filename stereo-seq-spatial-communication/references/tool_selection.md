# Tool Evidence Matrix

Use this as evidence for LLM reasoning, not as a fixed router. Match by sender/receiver design, spatial constraint, comparison question, and closest reusable article code.

## CellChat or CellPhoneDB With Spatial Filtering

Evidence pattern: broad communication between annotated bins/cells/domains where the main Stereo-seq value is spatial proximity.

Evidence:
- P07 constrained CellChat communication to within bin100 distance in labyrinth.
- P07 used CellPhoneDB for JZ/decidua interface bins after extracting interface regions.

## CellChat Comparison

Evidence pattern: communication networks are compared between conditions, treatments, layers, or disease states.

Evidence:
- P09 used CellChat v2.1.0 for layer-layer and cell-cell communication in NA, moderate AD, and severe AD.
- P10 used CellChat v1.4.0 for cell-population communication and pathway-level visualizations.

## Concentric or Radius-Stratified Analysis

Evidence pattern: interactions or cell proportions are interpreted around a focal pathology, high-stress area, virus-positive spot, or lesion.

Evidence:
- P09 defined concentric distance levels around high-stress focal points.
- P10 quantified immune-cell proportions in radius bins around virus-positive spots.

## NicheNet

Evidence pattern: a specific sender-receiver pair and ligand-target hypothesis is the focus.

Evidence:
- P05 used NicheNet for interactions between POSTN+ CAFs and SPP1+ macrophages.

NicheNet is not a complete spatial communication route unless combined with spatial filtering or a spatially defined sender/receiver design.
