# Validation Checks

- Confirm sender and receiver labels were validated upstream.
- Record whether communication is inferred from sc/snRNA, spatial bins, cellbins, or aggregated domains.
- Define spatial constraints before ranking interactions.
- Use physical distances when possible; otherwise report pixel/bin units.
- Avoid comparing networks with very different group sizes without normalization or caveats.
- Do not interpret LR expression as direct proof of signaling.
- Separate broad interaction discovery from focused ligand-target hypotheses.
- For condition comparisons, verify matched domains/layers and comparable tissue masks.
